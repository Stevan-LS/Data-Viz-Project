Global Water Resources Dashboard

Analysis of water resources, GDP, and human development across countries

Metrics Explanation

GDP per capita

Gross Domestic Product per person in current US dollars. Indicates economic output and standard of living.

Human Development Index (HDI)

Composite measure of health, education, and income dimensions. Ranges from 0 to 1, with 1 being the highest human development.

Water Use Efficiency

Value added per unit of water used (US$/m¬≥). Higher values indicate more efficient water use in economic activities.

$$ \text{Water Use Efficiency} = \frac{\text{Gross Value Added (US\$)}}{\text{Volume of Water Used ($m^3$)}} $$

Water Stress

Freshwater withdrawal as a proportion of available freshwater resources (%). Higher percentages indicate higher water stress.

$$ \text{Water Stress} = \frac{\text{Total Freshwater Withdrawal}}{\text{Total Renewable Water Resources}} \times 100 $$
